# Delete Your Account

If you'd like to delete your Voquill account, you have two options.

## From Within the App

1. Go to the **Settings** tab
2. Scroll down to the **Danger Zone**
3. Click the **Delete Account** button

## Via Email

You can also email us at [support@voquill.com](mailto:support@voquill.com) and we'll delete your account for you.
